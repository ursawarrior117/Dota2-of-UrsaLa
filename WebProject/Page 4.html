Game Basics

1) What is Dota 2?

Dota 2 is a MOBA(Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) game where you and four teammates choose from a set of different heroes to fight agaisnt the opposite team to destroy each other Ancient building.


Dota 2 Map

The Dota 2 map is divided into two sides, Radiant and Dire. The Radiant is on the left side The Dire lands on the right side of the map.

The map is also split into three lanes - top, middle, and bottom. The area between the lanes contains camps of neutral creeps, and is known as the jungle. Each team has three towers running down their side of each lane, which automatically attack enemy creeps and heroes within a certain radius.
